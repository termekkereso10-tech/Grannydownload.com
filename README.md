about granny

"Granny" is a popular mobile horror game where players are trapped in a creepy house by an aggressive, hearing-sensitive old woman (Granny) and must escape within five days by solving puzzles, hiding from her patrols, and avoiding sounds, with sequels adding Grandpa and Slendrina for more challenges, featuring jump scares, survival horror, and simple controls but sometimes buggy progress resets, making it a hit for teens who enjoy tense escape games. 
Gameplay Basics
Objective: Escape Granny's house in five days.
Mechanics: Stealth is key; any dropped item or noise attracts Granny, who rushes to investigate.
Hiding Spots: Use wardrobes and under beds to stay hidden.
Characters: In sequels like Granny 3, Grandpa shoots at you, and Slendrina can appear and instantly kill you with her gaze, requiring you to look away quickly. 
Key Features
Genre: Survival horror, action-adventure, single-player.
Availability: Playable offline on Android and Windows.
Monetization: Contains ads and in-app purchases.
Difficulty: Can be quite scary with jump scares, leading to nightmares for some, suitable for teens. 
Player Feedback
Players love the classic horror feel but sometimes get frustrated by bugs, like progress resetting when closing the app or the game freezing after being caught.
Suggestions for updates include multiplayer modes (playing as Granny or with friends) and new game modes.
